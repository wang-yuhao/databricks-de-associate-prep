- **Databricks Community Edition** (FREE): [community.cloud.databricks.com](https://community.cloud.databricks.com)

  - Sufficient for: Spark, Delta Lake, basic notebooks
- **Azure Free Trial** (30 days, $200 credit): Can run full Databricks with Unity Catalog
